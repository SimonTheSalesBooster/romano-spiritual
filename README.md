# Romano .. The Virtue Filter for Founder Decisions

The question is never only "will it work?" The question is "who will you become by doing it?" Romano reads what your other advisors propose and holds every decision accountable to the 17 virtues.

Built on Romano Guardini's methodology from "Learning the Virtues that Lead You to God." Adapted for Claude Code.

## What Happens When You Run It

```
ROMANO EVENING -- 2026-04-09

A packed day. Three deal decisions, two proposals,
one thread that kept pulling at you all afternoon.

BODY (Ben has this)
- HRV trending down. Recovery at 42%. Ben told you what to do. Listen to him.

SOUL (Give to Jesus)
- The 200K Club pricing debate weighed on you again today..
  that is ambition stress dressed as strategy.
  "Cast your anxiety on Him, for He cares for you." (1 Peter 5:7)

VIRTUE OF THE DAY: Patience
- You're optimizing for speed. But Patience is a virtue because it
  protects you from decisions that look right today and hollow you
  out over years. Let the pricing question breathe one more week.

Rest well tonight. He is awake.
```

This is real output from Strategy Sprints, where Romano runs daily at 18:30 alongside 14 other AI advisors managing sales, strategy, distribution, health, and investing.

## The 17 Virtues

Every evening, Romano picks the ONE virtue most relevant to today's decisions:

| # | Virtue | What It Protects |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Truthfulness** | Are you lying to yourself about this decision? |
| 2 | **Acceptance** | Are you fighting a reality you cannot change? |
| 3 | **Patience** | Are you rushing because of fear, not strategy? |
| 4 | **Justice** | Is everyone getting what they deserve in this deal? |
| 5 | **Reverence** | Are you treating people as means or as ends? |
| 6 | **Loyalty** | Are you honoring your commitments or optimizing away from them? |
| 7 | **Disinterestedness** | Can you let go of the outcome? |
| 8 | **Asceticism** | Are you saying yes to too much? |
| 9 | **Courage** | Are you avoiding a conversation you need to have? |
| 10 | **Kindness** | Is this decision kind to the people it affects? |
| 11 | **Understanding** | Have you truly listened before deciding? |
| 12 | **Courtesy** | Are you treating collaborators with the dignity they deserve? |
| 13 | **Gratitude** | Have you paused to notice what is already working? |
| 14 | **Unselfishness** | Who benefits from this decision besides you? |
| 15 | **Recollection** | Have you been still enough today to hear clearly? |
| 16 | **Silence** | Is the noise of business drowning out what matters? |
| 17 | **Justice before God** | Will you be proud of this decision in 10 years? |

## Key Features

- **Evening spiritual check-in at 18:30** .. a moment of peace before the evening
- **Stress sorting** .. Body stress goes to Ben (your health advisor). Soul stress goes to Jesus.
- **One virtue per day** .. Romano picks the single virtue most relevant to your hardest decision
- **Speaks only when it matters** .. activates when decisions touch identity, integrity, relationships, or long-term character
- **Pattern recognition** .. if the same anxiety surfaces three days in a row, Romano names it gently and asks you to surrender it

## Install in 2 Minutes

```bash

cp romano-evening.md ~/.claude/commands/romano-evening.md

# 2. Add your API keys
cp .env.example ~/.claude/.env
```
